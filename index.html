Self-Driving Car

Instructions

Your objective is to create the perfect self-driving car that will speed past and avoid all traffic.

You have 100 cars that are all making different decisions on how to avoid traffic using their 5 sensors.

When your car makes a good decision, hit the "save" button to save their brain. If they crash, hit refresh to try again with the brain of your previous save. Everytime your car makes a good decision, hit save again. If after several refreshes your car keeps crashing, hit the "trash" button to discard his brain and start over.

Creating the perfect car takes a lot of trial and error (as it should) so be patient and good luck!
